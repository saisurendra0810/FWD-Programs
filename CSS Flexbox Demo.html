Explanation

  • display: flex → Activates Flexbox layout.
  • justify-content: space-between → Aligns items horizontally with space between them.
  • align-items: center → Aligns items vertically at the center.

Common justify-content Values

  • flex-start – Items at the beginning
  • center – Items at the center
  • space-between – Equal space between items
  • space-around – Space around items
  • space-evenly – Equal spacing everywhere

Common align-items Values

  • flex-start – Top alignment
  • center – Center alignment
  • flex-end – Bottom alignment
  • stretch – Default stretch

Use Cases: Navigation bars, card layouts, dashboards, forms, and responsive designs.
